Projects across Sub-Saharan Africa

The Fairwater foundation in provides clean drinking water to millions of people in 15 countries across Sub-Saharan Africa. Scroll down to see the full list of projects.

Siaya - Kenya

In collaboration with the Osiligi Foundation, a total of 20 Bluepumps have been installed in the Siaya region of Kenya and continue to supply water to the local community.

South Sudan

In collaboration with various organizations including Oxyfarm, UNICEF and ICRC, a total of approximately 90 Bluepumps have been installed in South Sudan.
