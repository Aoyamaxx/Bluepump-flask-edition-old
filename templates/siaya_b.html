Providing clean drinking water to Siaya - Kenya

The Osiligi Foundation is a UK-based charity providing handpump rehabilitation in Kenya's Maasai region. 'Osiligi' is the Maasai word for 'Hope.' The Foundation is comprised of volunteers to reduce poverty in Kenya.

In 2019, the Osiligi Foundation installed Bluepumps in numerous regions across Kenya, including 20 Bluepumps in the Siaya region. With local cooperation, the Osiligi Foundation could safely store and arrange security for the Bluepumps.

The Osiligi Foundation prioritizes establishing virtuous relationships with communities, local authorities, and Technorelief, the authorized Bluepump distributor in Kenya. The Foundation established a Memorandum of Understandings (MOUs) with local communities and leaders, outlining each party's responsibilities.

In 2019, Technorelief provided a 'Training Phrase' to communities in which Bluepumps were installed, and a two-year maintenance agreement was established.
